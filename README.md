# 💳 B2B Financial Risk Analysis: Payment Liquidity Audit
**Focus:** Accounts Receivable Risk & Cash Flow Optimization

## 📊 Business Problem
In international B2B contracting, delayed payments create liquidity bottlenecks. This project identifies high-risk regions and payment terms that stall company growth.

## 💡 Key Insights (1,000 Invoices)
* **Total Cash Flow Risk:** **€11.3 Million** identified in "Late" or "Very Late" status.
* **High-Risk Region (Nordics):** Despite market stability, the Nordics showed the highest concentration of overdue payments.
* **The "Danger Zone":** The **90-day payment term** was the primary driver of "Very Late" status, suggesting a need for stricter 30-day credit policies.
